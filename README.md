# AMRlytics

Global antimicrobial resistance intelligence platform powered by WHO GLASS and ECDC EARS-Net surveillance data.

## What it does
- Surveillance dashboard across 66+ countries, 8 pathogens, 15 antibiotics
- Trend-based AI forecasting using Prophet time-series models
- Risk classification alerts (Critical/Extreme resistance, last-line drugs, 3-year acceleration)
- Country benchmarking against WHO regional averages, WHO BPPL 2024, and trajectory peers

## Data sources
- WHO GLASS (Global Antimicrobial Resistance and Use Surveillance System) — 2018–2023
- ECDC EARS-Net (European Antimicrobial Resistance Surveillance Network) — 2000–2024

## Disclaimer
AMRlytics is a surveillance and forecasting intelligence tool. It is not a clinical decision support system and does not provide medical advice.
